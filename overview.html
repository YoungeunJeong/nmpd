New Media Production and Design

Program Description

Students will create and produce media productions to give audiences content and experience. Students will learn how to develop and design the based web through comprehensive training at SAIT.

Your Career

Job opportunities after graduating can range from web design, corporate communications, digital signage and educational media, animation, simulation and game design.

Employment can be obtained in different places as unique new media production houses, agencies, corporate, educational, and government organizations. Graduates have opportunities to develop their businesses freelance or through small businesses.

There are many opportunities and fields that students can work in, such as

  • Graphic designer
  • Web designer
  • Corporate communications Digital signage specialist
  • Game designer
  • Digital signage specialist

Employment Information

  • Grad employment rate 91%
  • Median starting salary $39,000/ year
